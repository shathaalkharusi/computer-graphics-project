DDA Example



Draw a line starting at pixel (4,10) and ending at pixel (14,15) using DDA algorithm:


*step1: Calculate ΔX,ΔY,M from the given points


  1. ΔX=x2-x1=14-4=10
  2. ΔY=y2-y1=15-10=5
  3. M=ΔY/ΔX=5/10=0.5

*step2:Calculate numsteps,xinc,yinc


  1. numsteps=x2-x1=14-4=10
  2. xinc=ΔX/numsteps=10/10=1
  3. yinc=ΔY/numsteps=10/10=0.5
